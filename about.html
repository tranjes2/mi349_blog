About Me!

Hi, I’m Jessica! I am a full-time student, and I also work part-time waitressing at a Korean Restaurant. Aside from work and school, I have a variety of interests and hobbies such as drawing, knitting, online shopping, reading, and cooking.

I am also an avid watcher of TV, and often find myself lost in thoughts of the latest show I am watching. While I would not say I am a television critic, or have any experience in analyzing media at all, I do have strong opinions and thoughts about most of the shows and movies I watch. My favorite genre of television is anime; I have a long list of animes that I have watched and plan to watch. These are some of my favorites:

  • Attack on Titan
  • Hunter x Hunter
  • Food Wars
  • Fruits Basket
  • Jujutsu Kaisen

All in all, I am your run-of-the-mill college student that waitresses on the side. This blog is a place for me to write down my thoughts – mainly on media I watch – and share these opinions with my readers. In a way, it is just a fun place for me to publish these ideas, so I am hoping you find the topics I write on interesting!
